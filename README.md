# 📊 Sales Analysis 

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior during festive sales  
- Identify key demographics contributing to higher sales  
- Extract business insights to improve targeting and sales strategy  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- **Source:** Diwali Sales Dataset (CSV file used in notebook)  
- **Description:**  
  The dataset contains transactional sales data including customer demographics, product categories, and purchase amounts during a festive season.  
- **Key features/columns:**  
  - Gender  
  - Age  
  - State  
  - Marital Status  
  - Occupation  
  - Product Category  
  - Amount (Sales)

## 📊 Key Insights
- Majority of buyers are **female**, and they have higher purchasing power compared to men  
- Most active buyers belong to the **26–35 age group**, especially women  
- Top contributing states are **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka**  
- **Married women** show higher purchasing behavior  
- Customers working in **IT, Healthcare, and Aviation** sectors contribute the most  
- Most purchased categories are **Food, Clothing, and Electronics**
